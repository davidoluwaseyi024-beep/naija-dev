About the Conference

NaijaDevs Conference is Nigeria's leading software developer event, bringing together engineers, designers, product managers, and founders from every corner of the country. What started as a small meetup of 30 developers in Ikeja, Lagos in 2019 has grown into a national conference attended by professionals from all six geopolitical zones.

The theme for 2026 is "Build for Africa, Scale for the World." Our speakers will share real stories of how they built products in Nigeria - working around poor infrastructure, limited funding, and unreliable power - and still managed to ship software that millions of people use every day.

Whether you are a student writing your first line of code or a senior engineer at a global company, NaijaDevs 2026 has two full days of talks, workshops, and networking planned for you. Seats are going fast.
