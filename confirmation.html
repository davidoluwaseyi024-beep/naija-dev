Useful Developer Keyboard Tips

  • Press Ctrl + S to save your work. Do this often.
  • Press Ctrl + Z to undo. Output: 1 change undone.
  • To commit your progress: git commit -m "attended NaijaDevs 2026"
  • Use a clear variable name like attendeeRegistrationStatus instead of just x.
  • Press F12 to open browser developer tools - your best friend for this course.
